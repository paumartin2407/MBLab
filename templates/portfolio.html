People

Group Leader

Maria D (Lola) Martin-Bermudo completed her undergraduate degree in Biochemistry and Molecular Biology at the Universidad Autónoma in Madrid (Spain) and her PhD in Cell and Developmental Biology at Centro de Biología Molecular Severo Ochoa, Madrid (Spain). She then moved to UK to work in the laboratory of Nichola H. Brown, Gurdon Institute, Cambridge (UK) as a postdoctoral fellow. She became a group leader at the Department of Physiology, Development and Neuroscience at the University of Cambridge (UK), before being awarded a tenure track position from the Spanish Research Council (CSIC), at the Instituto de Parasitología y Biomedicina López-Neyra in 2000. In 2004, she moved to the CABD, CSIC-Univ. Pablo de Olavide, Seville (Spain).
